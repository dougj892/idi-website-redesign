Who We Are

A global advisory built for impact

IDinsight is a nonprofit data analytics, advisory, and research organization that helps development leaders maximize social impact. We sit at the intersection of rigorous evidence and practical action — bringing the best analytical tools to the decisions that matter most.

We work side-by-side with partners to design better programs, evaluate what works, and build the capacity to sustain evidence-informed decision-making long after we leave.

See Our Approach

Rigorous Evidence

We apply the gold standard of randomized evaluations alongside cutting-edge data science and machine learning.

Local Knowledge

Our teams are embedded in the communities where we work, bringing contextual insight that global evidence alone can't provide.

Actionable Impact

Every analysis is designed to drive decisions. We translate complex findings into clear recommendations that partners can act on.

Diverse Perspectives

We believe the best solutions come from diverse teams. Inclusion isn't just a value — it's central to the quality of our work.

What We Do

Methods built for the real world

We offer a full suite of data and evidence tools — from rapid surveys to longitudinal evaluations — tailored to what each partner actually needs.

01

Impact Evaluations

Rigorous randomized and quasi-experimental designs that establish causal evidence of what works — and what doesn't.

02

Data Science & AI

Machine learning, predictive analytics, and AI solutions that unlock patterns in data to inform smarter decisions.

03

Quick Turnaround Surveys

Rapid, high-quality data collection for time-sensitive decisions — delivering actionable insights when they're needed most.

04

Process Evaluation

Deep investigation into implementation quality and fidelity, revealing why programs succeed or fall short.

05

Monitoring Systems

Custom dashboards and data systems that give partners ongoing visibility into program performance.

06

Capacity Building

Training, mentoring, and institutional support that embeds evidence culture for the long term.
