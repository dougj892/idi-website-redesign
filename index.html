About IDinsight
"We sit at the intersection of
rigorous evidence and
practical action."

IDinsight is a nonprofit data analytics, advisory, and research organization. We help development leaders maximize social impact by bringing the best analytical tools to the decisions that matter most — not just in theory, but in the field, with real stakes.

We work side-by-side with partners to design better programs, evaluate what works, and build the capacity to sustain evidence-informed decision-making. We don't parachute in with pre-packaged solutions. We listen, we adapt, and we stay until the work is done.

Our methods

What we actually do

Randomized controlled trials and quasi-experimental designs that establish causal evidence of what works — and what doesn't. We design evaluations to answer the questions partners actually need answered, not just the questions that are easiest to study.

Machine learning, predictive modeling, and AI solutions that unlock patterns in data to inform smarter decisions. We build tools that teams can actually use — not black boxes that require a PhD to interpret.

High-quality data collection for time-sensitive decisions. When a government needs to know something next month — not next year — we design and execute surveys that deliver actionable intelligence fast, without sacrificing rigor.

Deep investigation into implementation quality and fidelity. We reveal not just whether a program worked, but why it worked — or why it didn't. That distinction matters when you're trying to scale what succeeds.

Custom dashboards and adaptive data systems that give partners ongoing visibility into program performance. We build for the actual users — field staff, program officers, ministers — not for data scientists.

Training, embedding, and institutional support that builds evidence culture from within. We succeed when our partners no longer need us — when the skills, systems, and habits we've built sustain themselves.
